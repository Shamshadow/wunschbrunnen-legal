2) Principle

The app "Wunschbrunnen" can generally be used without a user account. Personal data is processed only as required for app operation, in-app purchase processing, and delivery of non-personalized ads via Google AdMob.

3) Which data is processed?

a) Technical data (app operation)

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Language settings, timestamps
  • IP address (especially when communicating with third parties)

Purpose: provision, stability, and security of the app.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR and/or Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

b) In-app purchases (digital coins)

For digital purchases (e.g. coins), payment processing is done by Apple App Store or Google Play. We generally do not receive payment data (e.g. credit card information), but may receive:

  • Purchase/transaction status (e.g. "purchased", "refunded")
  • Receipt/confirmation data to unlock purchased content

Purpose: provision of purchased content, validation, fraud prevention.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

c) Advertising via Google AdMob (non-personalized)

The app uses Google AdMob for non-personalized ads. These ads are not based on a user profile or past behavior, but may use contextual information.

  • IP address
  • Device/app information (device type, OS, app version)
  • Interaction/measurement data (e.g. ad impression)
  • Technical identifiers/signals for fraud prevention and measurement

Purpose: ad delivery and billing, app financing, fraud prevention and basic measurement.
Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
